About Light

Where voices meet the stage.

Light is Madinah's first ever independent youth summit — a two-day experience built around dialogue, leadership, and real debate. We believe in giving young people a space that's entirely theirs.

Across six distinct committees, delegates tackle global issues with the energy and urgency they deserve. No scripts. No ceiling. Just ideas.
